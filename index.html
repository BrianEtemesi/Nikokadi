Deposit Money Using MPESA PAYBILL

  1. Go to M-Pesa Menu on your mobile phone
  2. Select Lipa MPESA
  3. Select PayBill
  4. Paybill No or Business No: 123445
  5. Enter NAME as the Account Number
  6. Enter Amount: Min KESH 10/=
  7. Enter Your MPESA Pin
  8. Wait for confirmation Message
